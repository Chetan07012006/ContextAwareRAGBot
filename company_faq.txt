ABC Customer Care - Frequently Asked Questions

1. Refund Policy

Customers can request a refund within 7 days of receiving their order. To be eligible, the product must be unused, undamaged, and returned in its original packaging. Refund requests should be submitted through the customer portal or by contacting our support team. Once the returned product passes inspection, the refund will be processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are credited back to the original payment method. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our mistake.

Products damaged because of misuse or normal wear and tear are not eligible for refunds. Customized or personalized products are also non-refundable unless they arrive defective.

2. Delivery Timelines

Standard delivery takes between 3 and 7 business days depending on the customer's location. Express delivery takes between 1 and 2 business days and is available only in selected cities.

Orders placed before 2:00 PM are usually processed on the same business day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM are processed the next business day.

Customers receive an email and SMS with tracking information once the order has been shipped.

Unexpected weather conditions, public holidays, or courier delays may increase delivery times.

3. Membership Program

ABC Customer Care offers three membership tiers:

Silver Membership
Silver members receive:
- Standard customer support.
- Early access to promotional offers.
- Monthly discount coupons.

Gold Membership
Gold members receive:
- All Silver benefits.
- Priority order processing.
- Free shipping on eligible products.
- Faster response from customer support.
- Exclusive seasonal discounts.

Platinum Membership
Platinum members receive:
- All Gold benefits.
- Premium customer support.
- Dedicated account assistance.
- Highest priority during support requests.
- Exclusive product launches.
- Priority refunds and replacements.

Membership status can be viewed in the customer account dashboard.

4. Account Cancellation

Customers can permanently close their account by visiting the Account Settings page and selecting "Close Account."

Before closing an account:
- All active orders must be completed.
- Any pending refund requests should be resolved.
- Membership benefits will immediately expire after account deletion.
- Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.

If a customer changes their mind before confirmation, they may cancel the request.

7. Order Tracking

Customers can track their orders from the Orders section of their account dashboard.

Tracking information is updated automatically every few hours.

If tracking has not updated for more than 48 hours, customers should contact customer support.

8. Password Reset

Customers who forget their password can click "Forgot Password" on the login page.

A password reset link is sent to the registered email address.

The reset link expires after 30 minutes.

9. Address Changes

Shipping addresses can only be modified before an order is shipped.

Once an order has been dispatched, the shipping address cannot be changed.

10. Warranty

Most electronic products include a one-year manufacturer warranty.

Warranty claims require:
- Original invoice.
- Product serial number.
- Description of the issue.

Physical damage caused by customers is not covered under warranty.

11. Exchanges

Products may be exchanged within 7 days if unused and in original packaging.

Exchanges depend on product availability.

If the requested replacement is unavailable, customers may choose a refund instead.

12. Privacy

Customer information is stored securely and is never sold to third parties.

Personal information is used only for:
- Processing orders.
- Providing customer support.
- Improving services.

Customers may request deletion of their personal information by contacting customer support.
